Mohamed Aboutrika

Mohamed Mohamed Mohamed Aboutrika محمد محمد محمد أبوتريكة born 7 November 1978) is an Egyptian retired professional footballer who played as an attacking midfielder and a forward. He came second in the African Footballer of the Year award in 2008 after Emmanuel Adebayor, and was one of five nominees for the 2006 award, and one of the ten nominated for the 2013 award.
